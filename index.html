Peijun Qing

About Me



Hi! I'm Peijun, a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Dartmouth College, working with Prof. Soroush Vosoughi and co-advised by Prof. Saeed Hassanpour. Prior to Dartmouth, I received my bachelor's degree from Xidian University, where I explored knowledge-augmented LLMs.


Research: I am generally interested in language model behavior, with the current focus on LLM alignment. Before that, I worked on improving robustness and efficiency of LLMs and optimizing complex reasoning. I am extremely interested in developing simple yet scalable methods to address intricate real-world scenarios.
