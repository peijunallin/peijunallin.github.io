Peijun Qing

About Me



Hi! I'm Peijun, a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Dartmouth College, working with Prof. Soroush Vosoughi. Prior to Dartmouth, I earned a B.S. degree from Xidian University. I began my journey in Natural Language Processing during an internship at OPPO Research Institute.


Research: I am generally interested in Language Model Behavior, with the current focus on LLM alignment. I am extremely interested in proposing simple methods to address complicated real-world scenarios.
